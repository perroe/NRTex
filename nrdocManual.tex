\section{Two ways to use the nrdoc class}
The \texttt{nrdoc} class can be used both by \LaTeX (using the
\texttt{latex} command) and pdf\LaTeX (using the \texttt{pdflatex}
command). Using \texttt{pdflatex} and including the option \textit{screen}
gives an \textbf{electronic report with hyperlinks}.

\subsection{Including a figure}
Figures are included by \texttt{includefigure}. An example is given
here:

\begin{verbatim}
\begin{figure}[ht]
  \begin{center}
    \includegraphics[width=8cm]{figs/fotball}
    \caption{\label{fig:logo} NR logo.}
  \end{center}
\end{figure}
\end{verbatim}
\begin{figure}[ht]
  \begin{center}
    \includegraphics[width=8cm]{figs/fotball}
    \caption{\label{fig:logo} NR logo.}
  \end{center}
\end{figure}
Note that the extension of the figure file name should be dropped. The
reason is that \LaTeX will find the required file itself. The figure
files should have the following format:
\begin{itemize}
\item \texttt{latex}: postscript [.ps] or encapsulated postscript [.eps]
\item \texttt{pdflatex}: .pdf or .jpeg/.jpg
\end{itemize}

\section{Citation using Bib\TeX}

Citations are handled by Bib\TeX{} and \texttt{natbib}. Some examples
are given in Table \ref{tab:cite}.
\begin{table}[ht]
\begin{tabular}{ l l l}
  \hline
  usual citations
  &  \cite{host02} & \verb!\cite{host02}!\\
  in ()           &  \citep{host02} & \verb!\citep{host02}!\\
  with a see      &  \citep[see][]{host02} &\verb!\citep[see][]{host02}!\\
  with a section/page & \citet[Section 2]{host02} & \verb!\citet{host02}!\\
  will all authors & \citet*{host02} & \verb!\citet*{host02}!\\
  without parentheses & \citealt{host02}&\verb!\citealt{host02}!\\
  only the year & \citeyear{host02} & \verb!\citeyear{host02}!\\
  only the author & \citeauthor{host02} &
  \verb!\citeauthor{host02}!\\
  \hline
\end{tabular}
\caption{Examples of citations using Bib\TeX{} and
  \texttt{natbib}. \label{tab:cite}} 
\end{table}
